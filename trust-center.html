Compliance

At Zettr, the security of your data is our top priority. We understand the importance of safeguarding sensitive information, which is why we adhere to the highest security standards in the industry.

  • PCI Certified: We are PCI DSS certified, which means we follow a comprehensive set of security standards designed to protect cardholder data. This certification ensures that all transactions on our platform are handled with the utmost security, giving you peace of mind.
  • Aligned with Visma Security Program: Our security framework is built in alignment with the Visma Security Program, a robust and comprehensive security initiative. By following this program, we adopt industry-leading practices and continuously monitor, assess, and improve our security posture to defend against evolving threats.
  • Continuous Monitoring and Improvement: Security is never a one-time effort. At Zettr, we constantly monitor our systems for vulnerabilities and apply patches promptly to protect against potential risks. Our commitment to security means we are always working to stay ahead of emerging threats.
  • Data Encryption and Privacy: All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, ensuring that your information is always protected. We employ strict access controls and regularly audit our systems to ensure compliance and security standards are met.
